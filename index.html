Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 24, 2025

Our Privacy Commitment

DropTimer is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from our app.

Data That Stays on Your Device

  • Timer settings and preferences
  • Theme selections and customizations
  • App usage preferences
  • Purchase status (handled by Apple)

What We Don't Do

  • No data collection or tracking
  • No analytics or usage monitoring
  • No data transmission to external servers
  • No third-party data sharing
  • No advertising or marketing data collection

Apple Services

In-app purchases are processed by Apple according to their privacy policy. DropTimer does not have access to your payment information.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our GitHub Issues page.
